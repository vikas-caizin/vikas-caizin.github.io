PRODUCT MANAGER

Role

At Caizin, we strive to create innovative products that meet the business need and are loved by the users. To grow our product offering, we are looking for an experienced product manager passionate about building products that customers love.

What will your job look like?
Drive the product and business-planning process across crossfunctional teams of the company. You will interact with clients, technical architects, and test engineers to design and implement solutions.
Appraise new product ideas and strategize appropriate to-market plans assist in Designing & delivering UI product features of high quality & on-time.
Be the customer advocate and bring as much intelligence around customer needs as possible to the table.
Analyse consumer needs, current market trends, and potential partnerships from an ROI and build vs. buy perspective.
Requirements
Exceptional writing skills with strong presentation and speaking skills.
Previous experience delivering finely-tuned product marketing strategies.
EA minimum of 4 years of experience in a dynamic product role.
A background in software engineering will be highly positive.

DEVOPS ENGINEER

Role

Responsible for implementing technical architectural and engineering DevOps solutions for AWS hosted products along with managing infrastructure effectively.

What will your job look like?
Intermediate to the proficient understanding of programming/scripting in any languages such as Java, Bash, Python, etc., meaning good at programming.
Work in Agile, collaborative environment to build, deploy, configure, and maintain systems, which may include software installations, updates, and core services.

Design and implement tools for automated deployment and monitoring of multiple environments.
Requirements
Excellent in solving problems; debugging; and designing & implementing solutions to complex technical problems.
Familiar with operating systems such as Linux, Windows, iOS, and Android.
Have an interest in, understanding of, or experience with Design Thinking Methodology.
Experience and knowledge with Agile Development Methodology.
